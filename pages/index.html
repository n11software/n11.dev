Data Privacy
We promise to never sell your data. You've probably heard that a million times, but we believe at N11 that your privacy is just as important as our privacy. We wouldn't want someone to leak our code, or our private information, why should we do that to our users? You can request your data at anytime, and we will send you an email with all of your data we can find on our servers. If at some point you decide you want any of this information removed or changed, please let us know, and we will protect your data. Learn more about how we protect your data.
Business Model
At N11 we've decided to use an "open-source" business model, meaning that everything we decide as a business will be available to the public, and anyone can contribute to it. This way, we can ensure that our business model remains transparent, fair, and open to everyone.
